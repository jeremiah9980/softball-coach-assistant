Getting started
Set up your API key and team settings in under 2 minutes
1
Open the app — visit jeremiah9980.github.io/softball-coach-assistant
2
Get an API key — go to console.anthropic.com, create a key starting with sk-ant-
3
Click "🔑 Set Key" in the top-right header, paste your key, check "Remember in this browser"
4
Click "⚙ Settings" — enter your team name, age group (e.g. 10U), and season label. This auto-fills every AI prompt.

How do I teach the drop ball grip to a 10U pitcher?
Drop ball — 10U fundamentals

Start with the 2-seam grip. Index and middle fingers sit across the narrow seams. Key coaching cue: "Snap your wrist down like you're throwing a frisbee into the ground."

3-step progression: (1) stand-still snap drills at 10 ft, (2) full-arm circle at half-speed, (3) game-speed with target at knees...
Ask a follow-up...
1
Click "+ New chat" in the sidebar to start a thread. The title auto-fills from your first message.
2
Ask anything coaching-related — mechanics, drills, situational play, mental game, parent communication. Full markdown responses.
3
Threads persist across sessions (saved in localStorage). Click any thread to continue the conversation where you left off.
Great starter questions: "What are the 3 most common hitting mistakes in 10U players?", "How do I run a parent pre-season meeting?", "Give me 5 drills for a player with slow hip rotation."

## CTX 10U Summer State — Coach Plan
### Pre-tournament prep
Finalize lineup by Wednesday. Primary: red jersey, white pants, red socks. Confirm Field 3 has dugout shade...

### Game 1 vs Sting 10U — 9:00am Field 3
Arrive 7:45 · Warm-up 8:00–8:45 · Lineup posted 8:45...
1
Fill the header fields — name, location, dates, format, uniforms, parking/gate info, food plan.
2
Add games with "+ Add game" — set day number, game time, arrive-by time, opponent, field number, uniform (Primary/Alternate).
3
Click "Save & generate itinerary" — AI writes a full coach plan + parent-facing weekend guide simultaneously.
4
Export: PDF (letter size, branded header), ICS (each game + arrive-by as calendar events with 45-min alerts, importable to Apple/Google/Outlook).
The ICS file includes a "Arrive by" event between the arrive time and game start, plus a 45-minute VALARM alert. Open in any calendar app — each game becomes its own event.
